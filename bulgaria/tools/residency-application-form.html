How to fill out the application form

Blagoevgrad application form

Download form

The application form has 6 pages in total. Many of the form fields require two entries: one in latin script plus a transliteration into cyrillic script.

Here's an example:

We suggest to fill out the form in two steps:

  • Fill out the form fields on the line with the English descriptions first. They are located below the lines.
  • Fill out the form fields on the line with the Bulgarian descriptions second. Either do it yourself or get help from a native speaker. Caution: An online transliteration tool for Russian won't help! Bulgarian cyrillic is different from Russian! If your like, book our convenient translation and checking service.
