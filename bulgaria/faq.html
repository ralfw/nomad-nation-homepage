FAQ

How long does it take to become a member?

We would like to day, „Just sign this form and you‘re done.“ And maybe that‘s how it can be in the future. But right now it‘s still taking a little longer, maybe a couple of days.The reason is that not everything has been sorted out internally. We are in startup mode, not serial production mode. Every new member is a pleasant surprise in many regards, also in terms of challenges. There are so many ways of doing business as a solopreneur. An online teacher of English is doing business in a different manner than an accountant, who is doing it differently than a contract programmer, or the owner of a software company hiring contract programmers.We still have to iron out what kind of solopreneur businesses we actually can accomodate. There will be limitations - but which exactly? As few as possible, but nevertheless we cannot reasonably cover all solopreneur businesses. Alas, we don‘t yet know which we can and which not. That‘s the reason we need some time to actually talk to you and internally assess your business.So much is clear however: The application process is much shorter and easier than setting up a self-employed business, let alone company in most countries.
